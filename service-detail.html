What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to act as artificial tooth roots. Once healed, a custom crown is attached, restoring both appearance and function.

At NeonSmile Dental, implants are planned using digital imaging and guided techniques to ensure accuracy, faster healing, and long-term success.

Who Is This Treatment For?

Dental implants are ideal for patients who:

  • Have one or more missing teeth
  • Want a fixed, non-removable solution
  • Have healthy gums and sufficient bone density
  • Prefer a natural-looking replacement
