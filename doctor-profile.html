Dr. Emily Carter

Prosthodontist & Implant Specialist

Dr. Emily Carter is a board-certified prosthodontist specializing in advanced dental implants, crowns, bridges, and full-mouth rehabilitation.

With over 12 years of clinical experience, she is known for combining precision-driven dentistry with a gentle, patient-centered approach.

Professional Background

Dr. Carter earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree and completed advanced prosthodontics training focused on implantology and restorative dentistry.

She regularly attends international dental conferences and continues her education in digital dentistry, guided implant surgery, and aesthetic restorations.

Areas of Expertise

  • Single & Multiple Dental Implants
  • Full Mouth Rehabilitation
  • All-on-4 Implant Systems
  • Crowns, Bridges & Veneers
  • Implant-Supported Dentures
